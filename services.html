Air Freight

We have cooperated with many leading airlines for years and an extensive network of dedicated partners across the globe to ensure the reliable and cost-effective delivery of your consignment. Transportation by air has become essential for both buyers and sellers. Our airfreight division is well-positioned to meet any challenge by air and can offer services like Tailor-Made Customer Service, Tracking and Tracing, Direct and Consolidated Services, Bonded Warehouse, Customs Formalities, and Clearance. We provide you with the best service in the world. It requires less time. Air transport provides comfortable, efficient, and quick services. It is regarded as the best mode of transport for shipping goods. We offer:

  • Consolidation Service
  • Charter Solutions
  • Customs Clearance and Brokerage

Sea Freight

We are an upcoming freight forwarding company. Our depth of knowledge provides us with a wide range of options in all modes of freight movement and customs procedures. We have extensive experience in general, marine, and manufacturing shipping. This puts us in a unique position to work with our clients to match their shipment, customs clearance, and documentation requirements with the right solutions. Sea freight is the more environmentally friendly mode of transport, and lead times are competitive compared to road freight. You might prefer sea freight to save money!

Ground Shipping

Ours is the perfect part and full load system for your direct Malaysian distribution. Due to our extensive network of branches and partners, we can ensure enough cargo space is available at all times. We also make sure your shipment is transported via the most efficient route and reaches its Malaysian and Singaporean destinations safely and on time. For you, this means maximum flexibility and planning security through individually agreed transit times and flexible departures.

Fresh & Frozen

Planning is everything in perishables logistics. Even with a perfect production line, product freshness can be compromised during delivery. For products with short shelf lives, supply chains must be resilient, ensure product longevity, and manage disruptions effectively. Cutting-edge technologies can now monitor container conditions in real- time, tracking factors like temperature, humidity, oxygen, and carbon dioxide levels.
